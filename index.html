Privacy Policy for Flap Flap

Last updated: 2 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TOREM ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with the mobile game Flap Flap (the "Game"). By downloading or playing the Game, you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, please don't use the Game.

Information We Collect

The Game does not require you to create a traditional account, and we never ask for your real name, email address, phone number, or precise location. The information involved is:

  • Username you choose. When you first play, you pick a display name. It can be anything you like and does not need to identify you. It is stored on our leaderboard server and shown publicly next to your score (see Public Leaderboard below).
  • Game progress. Your best score and a randomly generated player ID (a string of characters we create to tell players apart). The player ID is not linked to your identity or your device's advertising ID.
  • Multiplayer gameplay data. During a live race, your chosen username and your bird's in-game position are sent to our server in real time so other players in the same race can see you. This data is transient and is not stored after the race.
  • Connection data. Like any online service, our server may temporarily process your device's IP address to route network traffic and keep the service working and secure. We do not use it to build a profile of you.
  • Advertising data. The Game shows ads through Google AdMob, which collects information including your device's advertising identifier (Ad ID) and technical device and usage data. See Advertising below.

Some settings (sound and haptics preferences) are stored only on your device and are never sent to us. A device identifier is used only on your device to verify a locally-saved score hasn't been tampered with; it is not transmitted to our server.

How We Use Information

  • To run the game's core features: the leaderboard, your best score, and multiplayer races.
  • To display and rank scores against other players.
  • To show ads and keep the Game free.
  • To operate, maintain, and protect the service against abuse.

Public Leaderboard

The Game has a global leaderboard. The username and score you submit are visible to all other players of the Game. Please don't choose a username that reveals personal information you'd rather keep private. Your player ID is not shown to other players.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display rewarded ads. AdMob is provided by Google and may collect and use data — including your advertising ID and device information — to serve and measure ads, including personalized ads where permitted. We do not control what Google collects. You can learn more, and how Google uses this data, here:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How AdMob handles data

You can reset or delete your advertising ID, or opt out of ad personalization, in your device settings (on Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads). Where required by law (for example, in the EEA/UK), the Game will ask for your consent before showing personalized ads.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. Information is shared only:

  • With Google AdMob, as described above, to provide advertising.
  • Publicly on the leaderboard, limited to your chosen username and score.
  • Where required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users and service.

Data Retention

Your username, score, and player ID remain on our leaderboard server until you delete your data (see below). Advertising data is retained by Google according to its own policies. Transient multiplayer and connection data are not stored after they're used.

Your Choices and Deleting Your Data

You can permanently delete your data from within the Game at any time: Settings → Delete Account. This removes your username, score, and player ID from our leaderboard server and wipes the related data saved on your device.

If you'd prefer, or if you can't access the in-game option, you can request deletion by emailing us at the address below and we'll remove your data.

Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we'll delete it.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live (for example, the EEA, UK, or California), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your information, or to object to certain processing. You can exercise the core of these rights using the in-game Delete Account option, or by contacting us.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the limited information we handle. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we can't guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the "Last updated" date above. Significant changes may be announced within the Game.
